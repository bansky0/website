Guitar Valve Preamplifier

It is a project that unifies a 2- valve guitar preamplifier based on the Marshall (JCM800) and Fender (Champ 5E1) circuit designs. This work was published by AES Colombia as a chapter in the book “Avances de Audio en Latinoamérica“.

About me

My name is Jhonatan López and I live in Edinburgh, UK. Currently I’m studying my Master in Sound Design at Edinburgh University. As a sound and acoustic engineer, I’ve focused my skills in music production, composition and Bluetooth Speakers design. I’ve worked in lots of different areas of sound engineering including: Live Sound, Audio Recording, Sound Design for TV Shows, Video Games Music/FX, Music and Audio Editor/Mixer, and more!

I really enjoy making music with digital tools and play it online. As well as all that, I love drawing – some of my illustrations are posted in my Instagram account.
